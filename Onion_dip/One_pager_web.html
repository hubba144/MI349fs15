What is it?

This is an application that is used for artist to create and submit their art to the web and utilize the idea of self expression.

Description

This application can be utilized by both professional and unprofessional artists. However, it is mainly geared to those that are not necessarily aimed to receive recognition, but more of a stress reliever. It is where people are able to express themselves in various ways and can share it with others who enjoy the same hobby.
